Physician Profile

A performance-minded approach to foot and ankle care.

Dr. Bradley Makimaa is a foot and ankle specialist and President of Southernmost Foot & Ankle Specialists. His clinical focus includes sports medicine injuries, foot and ankle surgery, diabetic limb preservation, wound care, and urgent foot and ankle conditions.

As an athlete and Ironman World Championship competitor, Dr. Makimaa brings firsthand understanding of training, recovery, biomechanics, and the importance of returning patients to an active life.
